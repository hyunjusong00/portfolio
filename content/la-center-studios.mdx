## The virtual production stage

What surprised me most wasn't the physical sets. It was that they don't always need them anymore.

They have a virtual production stage where the entire set can be created digitally. You point a camera at a basic open space, and the system replaces it in real time with whatever environment you want. Lighting, depth, background, all of it moves naturally with the camera. You can shoot a subway station, an office on the 30th floor, or some version of New York that only exists as data.
